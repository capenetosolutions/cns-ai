# Project Brief: High-Ticket Store Building Ads

## Background
Client has built a successful dropshipping course. Now pivoting to high-ticket work—literally building stores for people who've taken the course. The kicker: clients have made money within 24 hours of getting their store.

## Service Details

**The Offer**: Done-For-You Shopify Store Build
- Client provides niche/scope
- Team builds complete store
- Launch-ready in [timeframe]
- **Result**: Store makes first sale within 24 hours

**Target Audience**:
- Previous course students
- People who want to skip learning curve
- Stuck/want results fast

---

## Ad Creative Needs

### Platforms
- Facebook/Instagram (primary)
- TikTok (short-form)
- YouTube pre-roll (if budget allows)

### Formats Needed
1. **Hook Video**: 15-30 sec (stop the scroll)
2. **Testimonial/Social Proof**: 30-60 sec
3. **Process/Behind-the-scenes**: 60 sec
4. **Carousel ad**: Static with animation potential

---

## Constraints

- Must comply with platform ad policies
- No income claims without disclaimers
- Focus on service (building), not guaranteed results
- Can mention "clients" but need release strategy

---

## Success Metrics

- CTR (click-through rate) > 2%
- CPC (cost per click) <$5
- CTA conversion: Book call / Buy
- ROI: 3:1 minimum

---

## Competitors to Research

- Ecomhunt
- Dropship Lifestyle
- Other DFY ecom services

## Questions for Client

1. **Can we feature the stores that made money in 24hrs?** (Need releases?)
2. **What's the actual investment?** ($497? $1997? $4997?)
3. **How many spots available?** (Scarcity angle?)
4. **Is there a guarantee?** (Refund/rebuild terms?)
