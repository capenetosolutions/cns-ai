## Part 1: Competitor Intelligence

### Key Competitors Researched:

**Dropship Lifestyle (Anton Kraly)**
- Angle: "Your own online business without inventory"
- Hook: Freedom lifestyle + high-ticket course
- Weakness: DIY focus, no DFY offer

**Ecomhunt**
- Angle: "Find winning products"
- Hook: Speed to market
- Weakness: Tool-focused, not service

**Other DFY Ecom Services**
- Angle: "We build it, you profit"
- Hook: Speed (48-72hr delivery common)
- Messaging: Outsource the setup headache

### Common Hooks in Market:
- "Start your online business today"
- "No inventory, no headaches"
- "Work from anywhere"
- Passive income promises
- "Done in 48 hours"

### What Works:
- ✅ Speed claims with proof
- ✅ Before/after transformation
- ✅ Real client results (not just screenshots)
- ✅ Risk reversal (guarantees)

### What Doesn't:
- ❌ Vague "make money online" claims
- ❌ Course-heavy selling (market saturated)
- ❌ Overpromising without proof

### Our Differentiation:
- **24 hours** (faster than competition's 48-72)
- **Real proof** (actual client stores making money)
- **From course creator** (authority + trust)
- **High-ticket positioning** (premium, not cheap)

## Part 2: Strategic Angles

### Angle 1: "The 24hr Store"
- **Hook**: "I built him a dropshipping store. 24 hours later, he made his first sale."
- **Why it works**: Specific time, real result, curiosity gap
- **Visual**: Dashboard proof → Client reaction → CTA

### Angle 2: "Course vs Done-For-You"
- **Hook**: "You can learn dropshipping... or you can skip to the part where it works."
- **Why it works**: Speaks to course graduates who haven't executed
- **Visual**: Split screen "DIY struggle" vs "DFY success"

### Angle 3: "The Test"
- **Hook**: "I told him to give me 24 hours. This is what happened."
- **Why it works**: Challenge format, creates narrative tension
- **Visual**: Countdown → proof → reveal → CTA

### Angle 4: "Why I Stopped Selling Courses"
- **Hook**: "I made more money BUILDING stores than TEACHING about stores."
- **Why it works**: Pattern interrupt + authority
- **Visual**: Course sales numbers vs store build revenue

### Angle 5: "The 5-Store Rule"
- **Hook**: "I'll only build 5 stores this month. Here's why..."
- **Why it works**: Scarcity, exclusivity, high-value perception
- **Visual**: Countdown spots + social proof
